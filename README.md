# Project Overview:
This project analyzes the Indian Premier League (IPL) matches data from 2008 to 2024 to uncover meaningful insights about teams, players, venues, and match trends.

# Dataset:
* Source: Kaggle — IPL Dataset
* matches.csv — Match-level data (teams, venue, toss, winner, result, etc.)
* Ballbyball_detail_2008_to_2024.csv — Ball-by-ball data for detailed player analysis

# Library Used:
* Numpy:For Numerical computations
* Pandas:For data manipulation and analysis
* Matplotlib:Data Visualization
* Seaborn:Statistical Data Visualization

# Analysis Covered
## Team Analysis:
* Most wins and losses overall across all seasons
* Toss wins and toss decision trends (bat vs field)
* Impact of winning toss on match outcome
* Home win percentage for each team
* Matches won by runs vs wickets
* Highest win by runs and highest win by wickets in IPL history
* Super over matches coun

## Player Analysis:
* Player with most Player of the Match awards (overall & per season)
* Top run scorers across all seasons
* Batsman with most boundaries (4s) and sixes (6s)
* Best strike rate batsmen (min. qualifying balls)
* Most wickets taken across all seasons
* Best economy rate bowlers
* Best death-over bowlers (overs 16–20)
* Most catches taken by a fielder
* Most common dismissal types

## Venue and city analysis:
* Venues that hosted the most matches
* Cities that hosted the most matches
* Best performing team at each venue

## Season-wise Analysis:
* Matches played per season
* Total balls bowled per season
* Total runs scored per season
* Orange Cap holder of each season
* Purple Cap holder of each season

# Key Findings;
* Mumbai Indians dominate IPL with the most wins (144), most toss wins, and highest total runs scored
* Delhi Capitals have the most losses (135) across all seasons
* Virat Kohli is the all-time leading run scorer in IPL history
* Yuzvendra Chahal leads all bowlers with 205 wickets — a spinner topping the charts
* AB de Villiers has won the most Player of the Match awards in IPL history
* Chris Gayle hit the most sixes and dominated POM awards in 2011 & 2012
* Mumbai is the most popular host city; Eden Gardens is the most used venue
* Winning the toss gives only a slight advantage — team performance matters more
* DJ Bravo is the best death-over bowler with 102 wickets in overs 16–20
* Mumbai Indians hold the record for the biggest win by runs — 146 runs vs Delhi Daredevils (2017)

# Conclusion:
This project explored 17 seasons of IPL data and uncovered meaningful patterns in team performance, player consistency, venue impact, and match trends. The analysis reveals that Mumbai Indians and Chennai Super Kings are the most dominant teams, while individual legends like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Chris Gayle have defined the batting era of IPL. On the bowling side, spinners like Yuzvendra Chahal have proven more effective than pacers in IPL conditions overall.
